How to install

  1. Tap Download the APK above. Allow the download if prompted.
  2. Open the downloaded Renter-Notify.apk from your notifications or Files.
  3. If Android blocks it, tap Settings → enable “Install unknown apps” for your browser or Files app.
  4. Tap Install, then open the app and scan your QR code.
